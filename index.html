1520 Cropper Road | Shelbyville, KY

About Property

This is one of finest Historical homes in Shelby County, known as the Chevy Chase this home is listed on the National and KY Historical Register. The original section of the the home built in the 1800's is still in pristine condition. The entry is out of a magazine with a curved staircase and the original hardwood floors. The home has an modern addition with a huge country kitchen with fireplace and family room with double sided fireplace leading into a sunroom with hot tub. This home has geothermal hvac, underground electric and water to the buildings out side and if you want more acreage than the 4.8 acres there is up to 80 more acres available the owners would consider selling.
